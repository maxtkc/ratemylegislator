SB1300 SD1 HD1 CD1
Measure Title: RELATING TO SCHOOL MEALS.
Report Title: Department of Education; Meal Subsidies; Appropriations ($)
Description: Beginning with the 2025-2026 school year, expands free school meal coverage to students who currently qualify for reduced-price lunch. Beginning with the 2026-2027 school year, expands free school meal coverage to students whose family income is not more than three hundred per cent of the federal poverty level. Appropriates funds. (CD1)
